Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 19, 2026

DrillReady ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of the schools, administrators, and staff members who use our app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights with respect to that information.

1. Information We Collect

DrillReady collects only the information necessary to operate the app:

  • Account information — username, display name, role (administrator or teacher), and an encrypted password that you create.
  • Student and staff records — names, grade assignments, and room locations that you enter manually within the app.
  • Drill session data — drill type, date, time, duration, and accountability status recorded during drills.
  • Device information — standard iOS diagnostic data collected automatically by Apple (crash logs, performance data). We do not have direct access to this data.

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Precise location data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Behavioral analytics or advertising identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected within DrillReady is used solely to provide the app's core functionality:

  • Authenticating users and managing role-based access
  • Storing and syncing drill session records, student lists, and staff information across your authorized devices via iCloud
  • Generating drill reports for your school's records
  • Sending local and push notifications related to drill activity

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage and Sync

All data you enter into DrillReady is stored in two places:

  • On your device — via local storage as a fallback when network access is unavailable.
  • iCloud / CloudKit — Apple's CloudKit service syncs your data across devices signed into the same iCloud account. This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not operate our own servers or databases. We have no independent access to data stored in your iCloud account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third parties. The only external service that handles your data is Apple's iCloud / CloudKit, as described above.

5. Children's Privacy

DrillReady is designed for use by school administrators and teachers. Student records entered into the app (names and grade assignments) are managed entirely by the school's authorized users. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13, and students do not create accounts or interact directly with the app.

Schools using DrillReady are responsible for ensuring their use of student data complies with applicable laws, including FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act) and COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as you use DrillReady. You can delete individual records (students, staff, drill sessions) from within the app at any time. To delete all of your data, you may delete the app from your device and remove the associated iCloud data from your device's Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. User passwords are stored using standard encoding practices, and data sync is handled exclusively through Apple's encrypted iCloud infrastructure. No security measure is perfect, and we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your DrillReady account.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the Effective Date at the top of this page. Continued use of DrillReady after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
